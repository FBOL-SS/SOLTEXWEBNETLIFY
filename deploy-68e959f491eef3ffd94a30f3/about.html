Our Story

Three decades of innovation in industrial sewing technology

Who We Are

Founded in 1992, SOLTEX LAGUNA has grown from a small workshop in Monterrey to the leading provider of industrial sewing solutions across Mexico and Central America.

Our team of 150+ engineers, technicians, and sewing experts are dedicated to developing machines that meet the exacting demands of modern textile production.

We combine German engineering precision with local manufacturing expertise to deliver reliable, high-performance equipment tailored to the Latin American market.

Our Timeline

1992

Founded as a small sewing machine repair shop in Monterrey, Mexico

1998

Launched our first proprietary industrial sewing machine model

2005

Expanded operations to Central America with service centers in Guatemala and Honduras

2012

Opened our current 25,000 sq ft manufacturing facility in Monterrey

2020

Introduced our DENIM PRO series with patented stitching technology

2023

Celebrated 30 years serving the textile industry with over 5,000 machines installed
